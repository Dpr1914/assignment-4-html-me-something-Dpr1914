  3. Arby's
  4. Pizza Hut*
  5. Macy's
  6. Arby's*
  7. Dunkin Donuts*

As you can see I have worked in the hospitality industry in some fashion for most of my career. I thing the reason why I stayed on this path for so long was my desire to own my own restaurant one day. I learned my on job work ethic from my first job at Dominoes Pizza located at 7240 Natural Bridge Rd, Normandy, MO 63121


I could see a clear picture for my career path as bright as day. However, there was an emptiness that I couldn't fill throughout jobs 3-7. Through typical office politics and nepotism I lost my desire to continue on the path I was on, and then Job 8(EDC) was dropped in my lap.

I literally opened my email one day from a hiring agency ask me to come in, I don't remember applying. This has to be best job I ever had, although, I can't but feel that I need to do something more.....

Best Trip Ever

Its 1992, 12 year old me has had a long year. From getting knocked unconsious while playing kickball to experiencing and dealing with the death of my mother, needless to say I needed a win and quickly before I shut down. Fortunately, my best friends family had the answer at the time, a 7-day Caribbean Islands' cruise. My vehichle on this journey was named the Carnival Ship Sensation. There were a lot of firsts that happend to me on that ship and around those Island's. The 7 islands visited were:

He's A Natural

Two things always came natural to me: drawing and math. Math has always been my second language as long I've known. However, drawing has always been my first love as a hobby. I can't remember when exactly the attraction came from, but by 3rd grade I was placing in art contesst without even trying with my drawing of Dr.Martin Luther King Jr.

From drawings, to paintings, to tattoos, I've evolved as an artist. At one point I considered a career in tattoing, but now, I just like it as a side hustle/hobbie
